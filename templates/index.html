PyBursa courses

Description

This course is designed for mastering web framework Django.

Education also includes:

  • Basic Pothon
  • HTML
  • CSS

In this course, we shifted the emphasis towards the practice. There is a moderate amount of theory - a maximum of practice. The approximate ratio of theory and practice: 30% to 70% Courses will take place from 7 March to 15 May. Practical lessons in the classroom + online lectures.
